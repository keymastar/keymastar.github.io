Recipe for perfect Homemade Pizza

Heat up those ovens

Dough Ingredients:
  • 5.63 oz Flour
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Quick Rise Yeast
  • 1/2 cup room-temp Water
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Granulated Sugar
Toppings:
  • 4oz Red Sauce
  • 4oz Mozzarella Cheese
  • 2oz Cheddar Cheese
  • 30g Pepperoni
  • 10 Green Olives
  • 2 Strips Thin-Cut Bacon
  • 1/2 tsp Fresh-Ground Rosemary
Instructions:
  1. Pour water into mixing bowl and sprinkle yeast evenly on top. Follow by sprinkling sugar on the yeast. Let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Pour in olive oil, flour, and salt. Mix until it forms a single, homogenous ball.
  3. Oil bowl and cover doughball in bowl with plastic wrap. Allow to rise for one hour, or until dough has doubled in size.
  4. Fold dough to remove the air. Allow for another rise like the last, or place in the fridge until using.
  5. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Farenheit. Roll out dough on a 14 inch baking sheet covered in cornmeal, to a quarter inch thickness.
  6. Spread sauce on dough, then sprinkle rosemary evenly. place toppings in any desired order evenly across the surface.
  7. Place pizza in oven and allow to bake for 15-18 minutes, or until the cheese starts to brown.
  8. Allow pizza to cool for 5 minutes before slicing and serving. 1/8th of pizza = 230 calories.
